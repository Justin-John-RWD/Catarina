Media Coverage of Property Dispute

June 2006, National Daily "La Prensa": Former Mayor of Catarina Accused of Corruption.


Former Mayor of Catarina Accused of Corruption

Businessman Jorge Giraldez Bernard joined Mr. Justin Dobson in publicly denouncing the actions of the former mayor of Catarina, accusing him of attempting to improperly sell disputed land.

August 2007, Esta Semana, Channel 8:
The respected weekly television program Esta Semana featured a 15-minute segment on the Catarina property dispute. The episode highlighted ongoing issues and public statements related to the case.

April 30, 2008: British Embassy Allegations Reported in "Nicaragua Hoy"


British Embassy Allegedly Criticizes Catarina Alcaldia
Nicaragua Hoy, April 30, 2008

The article references claims made by the mayor of Catarina about a letter from the British Ambassador. The letter, allegedly threatening diplomatic consequences if court rulings were ignored, was not made public, and its contents remain unverified.

June 16, 2008: National Daily "Hoy" Reports on Dobson's Documents


Mr. Dobson Presents Original Documents to Support Legal Claims
Nicaragua Hoy, June 16, 2008

Mr. Dobson presented documents including court-certified titles, purchase records, and evidence of Cooperativa's continued existence. The mayor of Catarina dismissed these documents, asserting authority over the property.
