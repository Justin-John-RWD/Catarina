Beginning of Conflict

In the late 1990s and early 2000s, there was increasing interest in properties surrounding Laguna de Apoyo, driving up property values. Members of the Gallegos family sought to capitalize on this rising interest.

Initially, Sr. Carlos Gallegos applied for a "Titulo Supletorio" over a 10.5 Mz lot used by local farmers. However, this application was contested by the farmers, who held legal title through the Cooperativa, and the court ultimately denied the application.

In 2001, Sr. Eddie Gallegos, then Mayor of Catarina, initiated another attempt to claim the land, leveraging his position and influence. In the name of the Alcaldia, he presented a "Declaracion Jurada" requesting a new Finca assignment from the Registrar for the same property. Despite procedural and legal inconsistencies, the Registrar complied, creating a new registry entry (Finca number 63447) in the name of the Alcaldia of Catarina on February 4, 2002.
