Introduction & Overview

The objective of this website is to document and share the details of an ongoing property title dispute in Nicaragua, which began in 2001. This account focuses on a foreign investor's experience navigating a property dispute involving local authorities and the legal system.

Update - January 9, 2012

On November 18, 2011, the Appeals Court of Masaya issued a ruling that overturned a 2010 lower court decision. This ruling instructed the Public Registry to cancel the inscription of the title transfer in the names of Mr. Dobson and his wife. This decision has raised concerns regarding its adherence to the Nicaraguan Constitution and the Law of Municipalities.

Background of the Dispute

This section provides a history of the dispute, from the original assignment of title by the Nicaraguan government to subsequent legal challenges. It details the various legal proceedings and the involvement of different governmental bodies in the case.
