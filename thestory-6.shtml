Court of Appeals Sentence

On June 22, 2004, the Court of Appeals for the department of Masaya handed down a sentence in favor of the Cooperativa Feliz Cruz Hernandez.

This sentence affirmed the Cooperativa's Dominion over the property and ordered the cancellation of the Registry Entry held by the Alcaldia of Catarina.
The cancellation was inscribed in the registry on July 2, 2004.

The result was not accepted by the Alcaldia who argued, publicly, that the Court of Appeals did not have authority over a Municipal Government. The Alcaldia vowed never to release the land and that they would seek other means to get title over the land.

With the sentence in hand, Mr Dobson and his wife sought all possibilities for a peaceful settlement via a number of different channels. At one point they had a meeting with the Mayor and presented proposals to the Mayor. One of the Proposals presented was the development of a New Mirador connected to the existing Mirador of Catarina.

However, the Alcaldia's response was that they had alternative proposals from others, citing, "the owner of Pharaohs Casino".
