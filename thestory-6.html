Court of Appeals Sentence

On June 22, 2004, the Court of Appeals for the Department of Masaya issued a ruling in favor of the Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez.

The ruling affirmed the Cooperativa’s ownership of the property and directed the cancellation of the registry entry held by the Alcaldia of Catarina. This cancellation was officially recorded in the registry on July 2, 2004.

Despite this decision, the Alcaldia publicly contested the Court of Appeals’ authority, arguing that it did not have jurisdiction over a municipal government. The Alcaldia expressed their intent to seek alternative methods to claim title to the property.

In an effort to find an amicable resolution, Mr. Dobson and his wife explored several options for a peaceful settlement. This included meetings with the Mayor of Catarina and the presentation of development proposals, such as a plan for a new mirador connected to the existing Mirador of Catarina.

However, the Alcaldia indicated that they were considering alternative proposals from other parties, including one reportedly from the owner of Pharaohs Casino.
