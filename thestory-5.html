Arrival of Foreign Investors

In 2002, Mr. Justin Dobson and his wife, Claudia Pacheco, became involved in the property dispute in Catarina. While searching for properties in the area, they discovered this property listed for sale by the Alcaldia of Catarina. Upon further investigation, they learned that the property’s ownership was under dispute and that the Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez were the rightful owners.

Mr. Dobson and his wife conducted an in-depth investigation, uncovering evidence of irregularities in the Alcaldia’s handling of the property. Recognizing the Cooperativa’s legitimate ownership, they entered into an arrangement to support and fund the Cooperativa’s legal efforts.

Shortly after their involvement, the President of the Cooperativa was convicted on charges of trespass and sentenced to jail. Mr. Dobson and his wife stepped in to provide support for his family and worked through the courts to secure his release.

During this time, information surfaced regarding a prior arrangement between the Mayor of Catarina, Sr. Eddie Gallegos, and Mr. Jorge Giraldez Bernard. Mr. Giraldez had allegedly made a down payment for the property, pending the outcome of the Cooperativa’s lawsuit. After learning more about the dispute, Mr. Giraldez concluded that his agreement with the Mayor was not legitimate and aligned himself with the Cooperativa and Mr. Dobson.

Mr. Dobson and his wife finalized their purchase of the property from the Cooperativa on April 15, 2003, with the transfer officially registered in Masaya on September 2, 2004.
