Arrival of Foreign Investors

In 2002, Mr Justin Dobson and his wife, Claudia Pacheco, entered the picture. Justin and his wife had been looking at properties in the Catarina area and had been shown this property as "for sale" by the Alcaldia of Catarina. Upon investigation they learned that the property was in dispute and that the rightful owners were the Cooperativa, and not the Alcaldia.
A thorough investigation unearthed a great deal of information as to the fraud being perpetrated by the Alcaldia.

Mr Dobson and his wife entered into an arrangement with the Cooperativa to support and fund the legal process.

Soon after closing this, the President of the cooperativa was convicted on trumped up charges of trespass and sentenced to jail. Justin and his wife stepped in to support his family and to win his freedom through the courts.

During the investigation conducted by Mr Dobson, information came to light about a deal having been struck between the Alcalde (Sr. Eddie Gallegos) and a Mr Jorge Giraldez Bernard. According to Mr Giraldez, a down payment had been paid, on the purchase of the property, to the Mayor (Eddie Gallegos), and he was awaiting the results of the Cooperativa Lawsuit in order to conclude the final purchase from the Alcaldia.

Mr Giraldez now believes that his "deal" with Eddie Gallegos was a fraud and now supports the position of the Cooperativa together with Mr Dobson and his wife. See La Prensa Interview with Mr Jorge Giraldez

The final purchase of the Property by Mr Dobson and his wife from the Cooperativa was concluded April 15, 2003. The transfer was registered in Masaya September 2, 2004.
