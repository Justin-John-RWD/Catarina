Intimidation Tactics


Pedro Pavon, President
Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez

In order to intimidate the Cooperativa into abandoning their lawsuit, the Alcaldia of Catarina brought criminal trespass charges against the President of the Cooperativa (Sr. Pedro Pavon), for entering his own (the Cooperativa's) land.

Without due process and without an opportunity to mount a defence, the Local Judge (Catarina) found Sr. Pavon guilty and sentenced him to one year in jail. Sr. Pavon is aging, infirm and supports a large family. With Sr. Pavon in jail, the family were left defenseless. The Alcaldia then sent people to explain to the other members of the Cooperativa that Sr. Pavon would be set free if they backed off from the lawsuit.

The Cooperativa and their families were also subject to other forms of organised discrimination. All were prevented from holding jobs within the local community.
