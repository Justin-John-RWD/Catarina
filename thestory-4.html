Intimidation Tactics


Pedro Pavon, President
Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez

During the ongoing legal dispute, the Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez faced significant pressures. The Alcaldia of Catarina initiated criminal trespass charges against the Cooperativa’s President, Sr. Pedro Pavon, accusing him of entering the land owned by the Cooperativa. Sr. Pavon was convicted by the local court without adequate opportunity to present a defense and sentenced to one year in jail.

This decision had a profound impact on Sr. Pavon’s family and the Cooperativa. As an elderly individual supporting a large family, his imprisonment created significant hardship. Members of the Alcaldia allegedly approached other members of the Cooperativa, offering to secure Sr. Pavon’s release if they agreed to abandon the lawsuit.

In addition to legal challenges, the Cooperativa and their families faced social and economic pressures, including being excluded from local employment opportunities.
