Latest Developments/Current Status
Updated August 2011

Status: Awaiting Appeals Sentence on "Cancellation of Title" case brought by Alcaldia of Catarina and PGR against Justin Dobson and wife. District Judge issued sentence in favor of Dobson & Wife in 2010.
The Eviction ordered for April 2008 has not yet been carried out

Regarding Eviction case: The District Judge finally ruled that the Government of Nicaragua (PGR) has no right to Intervene in the Eviction case. The PGR immediately filed asking the judge to re-consider ("reposicion de auto"). The Judge again ruled against the PGR.

New case: Within days the Alcaldia of Catarina joined by the PGR filed a new lawsuit against Justin Dobson and his wife seeking cancellation of the property title. They also asked that the District Judge be recused.

Finally last year, around August 2010, the substitute judge issued a sentence in favor of Dobson and his wife and awarded costs. This sentence was appealed to the Magistrates Tribunal of Masaya.

In January 2011, the Appeals court issued a notification to parties of an imminent Sentence. As of August 2011, the sentence has not been issued.
