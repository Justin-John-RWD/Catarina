Lawsuit Seeking Eviction and Restitution

Due to the lack of resolution with the Alcaldia, Mr. Justin Dobson initiated a lawsuit requesting the eviction of the Alcaldia's "caretakers" and the restitution of the disputed property.

In January 2008, the District Court of Masaya delivered a final ruling in favor of Mr. Dobson. Subsequently, in March 2008, the court issued a Sentence Execution Order (Ejecutoria), formally instructing the restitution of the property to Mr. Dobson.

The District Judge of Masaya also issued an Order for Eviction with Police Assistance, scheduling the eviction for April 18th at 9:00 AM. All relevant parties were duly notified.

To secure the property, armed security was hired, and a workforce of over 60 individuals, including members of the Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez, was assembled. These measures were taken to ensure the smooth transfer of possession as directed by the court.
