Lawsuit seeking Eviction and Restitution

Without change in the attitude of the Alcaldia, Mr Dobson commenced a new lawsuit seeking eviction of the Alcaldia's "caretakers" and Restitution of the property.

In January, 2008, the District Court of Masaya issued a final sentence in favor of Mr Justin Dobson.

In March 2008, the District Court of Masaya issued the Sentence Execution Order (Ejecutoria), ordering Restitution of the property into the hands of Mr Justin Dobson.

Finally the District Judge of Masaya issued an Order for Eviction with Police Assistance, for April 18th at 9.00am (see sidebar). All parties were notified.
Armed Security was contracted and a workforce of more than 60 men recruited, including members of the Cooperativa Felix Cruz Hernandez, to assist in securing the property once the Judge officially transferred Possession to Justin Dobson.
